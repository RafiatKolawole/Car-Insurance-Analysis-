# Car Insurance Claims Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project analyzes car insurance claims data to identify patterns in customer behavior, claim frequency, and key risk factors that influence both claim occurrence and claim cost.

The dashboard consolidates key insurance metrics and explores how demographic, vehicle, and geographic factors impact claims.

## Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel
- Data Cleaning & Preparation
- Data Analysis
- Dashboard Design & Visualization

## Objectives
- Analyze total claims and average claim amounts
- Identify car brands and models with the highest claim frequency
- Examine how demographic factors affect claim behavior
- Assess the impact of coverage zones on claim costs
- Explore the relationship between income level and claim behavior

## Key Insights
- Claim patterns varied between male and female drivers, indicating differences in vehicle usage and risk exposure
- Certain car makes and models recorded higher claim frequencies
- Urban coverage zones showed higher average claim amounts compared to suburban and rural areas
- Income level demonstrated a relationship with overall claim behavior and frequency

## Conclusion
This analysis highlights how customer demographics, vehicle characteristics, and location contribute to insurance risk. The project strengthened my Excel-based data analysis, visualization, and dashboard-building skills.
